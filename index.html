    Accelerate Employee Productivity

    Obie delivers expert-verified knowledge through a self-serve gateway in Slack. Consolidate access to all frequently asked questions, documents, onboarding-flows and your company knowledge base.

    We’ve also put Obie right in the places you work including Slack, Confluence, Jira Service Desk, Google Drive,Dropbox and much more. Setup is a breeze.

    Capture Knowledge from Conversations

    Obie becomes an intelligent knowledge base that will help document internalknowledge and make it instantly accessible from wherever employees work.More than just an internal knowledge base, Obie serves as each employee’s virtual support agent to scale success and productivity.

    With built-in authoring tools, Obie enables fast and rich knowledge sharing. Integrate the information you already have into one source of truth with ease and privacy.

    Streamline internal support

    Obie helps you build an internal FAQ system, streamline your process of onboarding employees discover trends, common questions, and employee requests. Use this data to influence business decisions and shape knowledge creation. Obie enables your team to do the best work by proactively ensuring everyone has access to the most up-to-date information.
